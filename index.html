About the Corps

Our Mission

The Demon Slayer Corps is an organization that has existed since ancient times, dedicating its existence to protecting humanity from demons. We seek brave individuals willing to undergo rigorous training to master the art of demon slaying.


Our members use specialized breathing techniques and Nichirin Blades forged from a unique ore that absorbs sunlight - the only known weakness of demons besides wisteria.
